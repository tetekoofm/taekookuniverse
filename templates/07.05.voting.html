What is Voting?

Voting is one of the many ways for fans to show how much Taehyung and Jung Kook's music are loved and supported. They help them gain their well-deserved recognition.

Why is Voting Important?

  • Recognition: Votes help artists to win awards, showcasing their talent and hard work to the world.
  • Global Impact: Higher vote counts amplifies their presence globally.
  • Ensuring Future Nominations: Winning fan-voted awards makes them eligible for future nominations and bigger achievements.
  • Fan Power: Voting shows the strength and unity of the fandom, proving that we are here to support them. Together, we can help Tae and Koo achieve even greater heights!

Types of Voting

Voting can be done either through an app or website poll. There are also different kinds of votings as follows:

  • Pre-Voting: Conducted before an award show with a list of nominations from which top ranks are chosen for Live voting.
  • Live Voting: Takes place during the award show in real-time and the winner is awarded.
  • Timed Voting: Some platforms allow voting within a timeframe, as in, once per day per account or once per hour etc.,
  • Unlimited Voting: Certain platforms allows to vote as many times as possible.

How to Vote?

Follow these steps for doing it the right way:

  • Step 1: Find the voting platform where Tae or Koo is nominated. Make sure they are an official platform.
  • Step 2: Create an account with the app or website using a valid email. Some platforms require email verification, so always make sure to create with a valid email address.
  • Step 3: Go through the platform specific guidelines and follow them to the T.
  • Step 4: Find the appropriate category where they are nominated and cast your vote.
  • Step 5: Spread the word, use hashtags and share them on social media to encourage others to participate.
  • Step 6: Our beloved fanbase, TK VT has prepared a TK Voting Guide, a detailed step-by-step voting instructions for every platform. Please utilize it!
  • Step 7: Join our hardworking Voting Team who works day and night to help Tae and Koo secure a win. Reach out to Taekook Universe to join the voting group on X.
  • Step 8: DO NOT use VPN or use macros for voting. Some platforms disqualify votes if they detect bot-like behavior. SO, always do it the right way!
  • Follow the guidelines from our fanbase.
  • Reach out to Taekook Universe for any questions.
  • Avoid suspicious activities that might get your votes disqualified.
  • Use multiple accounts responsibly. Do not spam!
  • Engage in team voting projects.
