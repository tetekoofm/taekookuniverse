Why Donate?

As fans, there are many ways we can support Taehyung and Jungkook, and one of the easiest is through donations. These donations are used solely for fan-led initiatives such as:

  • Giveaways: We organize events and do giveaways for fans to celebrate new releases and milestones.
  • Streaming Parties: Funds are used to sponsor premium accounts on streaming platforms, which, in turn, help boost the streams.
  • Album Purchases: Donations are used to buy their singles or albums on platforms like iTunes, Amazon Music, Qobuz, and others to increase chart positions.
  • Voting Campaigns: We organize drives to sponsor memberships on voting apps, helping the artist and their songs reach #1 on various platforms.
  • Charity Projects: Occasionally, donations are collected for charity projects benefiting communities, all in the name of Taehyung and Jungkook.

Every penny goes directly to the benefit of Taehyung and Jungkook, ensuring they reach new heights in their careers. We maintain transparency by saving receipts for every transaction, which are available upon request.

Donation Methods

We offer two convenient ways for fans to contribute. Whether you prefer making a one-time donation or supporting long-term efforts, your contribution will help us make a big impact!

Please choose Friends & Family while making donations.

  • PayPal: For direct and secure donations, donate via PayPal.
  • Ko-Fi: For easy one-time or recurring monthly donations, donate via Ko-Fi.

Frequently Asked Questions

How will I know my donation is being used appropriately?

Transparency is key, so we provide detailed records of all funds raised, along with receipts for purchases and campaigns. Every contribution under our control is carefully directed toward projects that support both artists, with no favoritism.

Can I donate for a specific project?

Yes, when a specific project or initiative is being funded, we will provide a clear donation goal, and your donation will go toward that specific project.

Is there a minimum donation amount?

No, any amount is greatly appreciated. Every bit counts, and small donations add up to make a big difference!

Will my donation be tax-deductible?

As this is a fan-led initiative, donations are not tax-deductible. However, we ensure transparency and provide receipts for all contributions.
